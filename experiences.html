Signature Experiences

Shangri-La · Yunnan

Dawn at Songzanlin

The Wild-Mushroom Table

Where You Stay

Stays that belong to the journey.

We do not choose hotels simply because they are five-star. We look for character, a strong sense of location and the level of service the journey deserves.

Beijing

The Peninsula Beijing

Set on Wangfujing in the heart of the capital, a short walk from the Forbidden City. A sweeping renovation created some of the most generous rooms in Beijing, finished in a calm, residential style, with celebrated Cantonese dining at Huang Ting. An ideal base between rounds at the capital's leading courses.

Shanghai

Waldorf Astoria Shanghai on the Bund

Occupying No. 2 on the Bund, with the storied 1911 Shanghai Club building — and its famous Long Bar — at its heart. Restored colonial grandeur, a contemporary tower above the Huangpu, and the championship golf of Sheshan a straightforward drive away.

Lijiang · Yunnan

Banyan Tree Lijiang

Stone-walled villas and private courtyards at 2,400 metres, built in the local Naxi style on the edge of Shuhe Ancient Town, looking directly onto the peaks of Jade Dragon Snow Mountain. Traditional architecture, mountain air and unhurried service between rounds beneath the snow line.

Lijiang · Yunnan

Jinmao Purelax Lijiang

Part of The Unbound Collection by Hyatt, at Ganhaizi meadow on the slopes of Jade Dragon Snow Mountain — the closest luxury base to the peak itself. Every room opens onto a private balcony facing the mountain, and the world's longest golf course is minutes away.

Hainan

Hainan Island Hideaways

Beachfront villas and quiet coves within easy reach of the island's finest courses — the likes of Rosewood. In Beijing, restored hutong courtyard stays put imperial Beijing outside the door.

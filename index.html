Why Golf China

The golf trip almost no one has taken.

Most international golfers don't think of China first — and that is precisely the point. The courses are world-class; the settings are unlike anywhere else on earth.

01

A destination most travelers have never seen

China isn't the obvious golf trip — that is exactly why it feels different. You'll play courses your friends have never heard of, let alone walked.

02

Landscapes you cannot recreate elsewhere

Great Wall mountains, Yunnan snow peaks, volcanic lava fields and tropical coastline — all within a single country.

03

Culture between every round

Not just resort golf. Food, history, old towns, tea roads and private guides give each day a sense of place.

Fully handled on the ground

Tee times, drivers, hotels, translation, payments, entry guidance and plans for non-golfers — all taken care of.

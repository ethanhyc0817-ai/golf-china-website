Signature Journeys

Curated routes, told day by day.

Every journey pairs championship golf with the soul of each place — pre-booked tee times, private transfers, hand-picked hotels and a personal guide throughout. Each is a starting point, fully tailorable to your pace and handicap.

6 Days
Beijing

The Imperial North

The capital's history and grandeur, with golf in the shadow of the Great Wall.

  • Day 1Arrive Beijing; settle into a courtyard-style suite. Evening: a first taste of the capital over Peking duck.
  • Day 2Golf at Reignwood Pine Valley (Jack Nicklaus), set against the Jundu Mountains; afternoon, a private walk on a wild, restored stretch of the Great Wall at golden hour.
  • Day 3The Forbidden City before the crowds with a historian; lunch in a hutong courtyard; afternoon at leisure or a second nine.
  • Day 4A second championship round; later, a tea master and the cypress groves of the Temple of Heaven.
  • Day 5Contemporary Beijing — the 798 art district and a Michelin-starred dinner.
  • Day 6Private transfer for your onward flight, or connect to Jiangnan Greens.

6 Days
Shanghai & Hangzhou

Jiangnan Greens

Cosmopolitan Shanghai and the misty water-towns of the lower Yangtze.

  • Day 1Arrive Shanghai; sunset on the Bund, the skyline mirrored across the Huangpu.
  • Day 2Golf at Sheshan International, host of the WGC-HSBC Champions; evening in the French Concession.
  • Day 3A links round at Yangtze Dunes, where the river meets the sea; afternoon, classical gardens and a bespoke tailor.
  • Day 4High-speed train to Hangzhou; West Lake by private boat at dusk.
  • Day 5Golf at Fuchun Resort, fairways reflected in still water beneath misty hills; spa and Longjing tea villages.
  • Day 6Onward transfer, or connect north to Beijing.

7 Days
Yunnan

The Southwest Highlands

Eternal-spring climate, snow peaks and ethnic-minority culture — China at its most distinctive.

  • Day 1Arrive Kunming, the City of Eternal Spring.
  • Day 2–3Two rounds at Spring City — the Nicklaus Mountain course and the Robert Trent Jones Jr. Lake course, ranked among China's very finest.
  • Day 4Fly to Lijiang; the UNESCO old town and Naxi culture at twilight.
  • Day 5Golf at Jade Dragon Snow Mountain — the world's longest course, beneath a 5,500m glacier.
  • Day 6Tea-Horse Road villages, a farmhouse lunch and the Black Dragon Pool.
  • Day 7Onward transfer.

8 Days
Guilin & Hainan

Karst & Coast

The scroll-painting landscape of Guilin, then golf along China's tropical island shore.

  • Day 1Arrive Guilin, among the limestone peaks of the ¥20 note.
  • Day 2Golf at Guilin, karst spires rising straight from the fairways.
  • Day 3A slow cruise down the Li River to Yangshuo; cycle between the rice paddies.
  • Day 4Fly to Hainan; arrive on the untouched tropical east coast.
  • Day 5Golf at Mission Hills Blackstone, carved from black volcanic rock.
  • Day 6A links round at The Dunes, Shenzhou Peninsula, on natural sand dunes by the sea.
  • Day 7Oceanfront golf at Shanqin Bay, then the beach and a seafood feast.
  • Day 8Onward transfer.

The wild-mushroom table

Into the pine forests with a master forager to hunt matsutake and porcini — then watch the morning's haul become dinner at a local chef's table.

Lijiang · Yunnan

Tea on the Tea Horse Road

Aged pu'er poured by a tea master in a Naxi courtyard along the old caravan route — a thousand years of trade in one cup.

Hangzhou

West Lake by private boat

A quiet wooden boat and an oarsman who has worked these waters for decades — on the lake at dawn, before it belongs to anyone else.

Who We Are

Built by people who live between two worlds.

Golf China began with a simple observation: the golfers who know the world's great courses almost never know China's — not because the golf isn't there, but because the clubs are private and no one had opened the doors. So we became the first golf travel company dedicated to China, and built the relationships ourselves.

Our team grew up here, plays here, and has spent years moving international travelers through China. Through our exclusive club relationships and a long-standing partnership with China Tourism Group, we have people on the ground in every destination we send you — handling the clubs and the visas, but also knowing which courtyard pours the best pu'er and which stretch of the Wall is empty at sunset. Every enquiry is answered personally, by someone who has walked the route you'll travel.

— The Golf China team

Who We Are

We built the company China golf was missing.

Golf China

China's first golf travel company.

China has extraordinary golf, but planning a serious golf journey here has never been straightforward. The best courses can be difficult to identify, private-club access is rarely public, and the distances between destinations make local knowledge essential.

Golf China was created to solve that. We work directly with leading clubs across the country, design every route around first-hand knowledge and manage the journey from arrival to departure. The relationships, the access and the routes were not borrowed from somewhere else. They were built here.

Every enquiry is answered personally by someone who knows the courses, destinations and routes being discussed.

— The Golf China Team

Signature Courses

Courses worth crossing the world for.

Each has the landscape, architecture and sense of place to anchor an entire journey.

China's finest courses run beneath the Great Wall, through the highlands of Yunnan, across ancient lava fields and along Hainan's tropical coast. Several have ranked among the world's best — Sheshan International, Spring City and Blackstone have all appeared in Golf Digest's World's 100 Greatest.

Many are private and beyond the reach of most international golfers. Through direct, long-standing club relationships, we arrange guest play at courses rarely experienced by overseas guests — private-club access by arrangement on every journey.

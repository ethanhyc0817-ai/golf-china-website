The Journey

From a glacier to the sea, in nine unhurried nights.

No other golf trip covers this much altitude. It opens beneath a 5,500-metre glacier in the Himalayan foothills and closes barefoot on a tropical coast — with Spring City's World Top 100 fairways and Blackstone's lava fields in between.

The pacing is deliberate: two days in Lijiang before the first tee shot, because at 3,100 metres the round is better for a night or two of acclimatising. Then the journey descends — to Spring City's highland lake, where you stay inside the resort itself, and on by direct flight to Hainan.

Hainan is the second act: Blackstone carved through black lava, a free day among volcano villages and spa pools, and a closing links round at The Dunes — your last night beside the course and the sea.
